Viega Project

Pond Rejuvenation in BKT — Restoring ecosystems and water security

Project Overview

The Viega Project focuses on the ecological and hydrological restoration of neglected village ponds in the BKT (Bakshi Ka Talab) area of Lucknow. Interventions include pond dredging to remove silt buildup, bund repair, inlet/outlet channel restoration, and native flora planting to support biodiversity and increase the local water table for agriculture.
