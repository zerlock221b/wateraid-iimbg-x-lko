Bayer Project

Improving WASH infrastructure at Anganwadi Centres across Lucknow

Project Overview

The Bayer Project focuses on rehabilitating Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) infrastructure at Anganwadi Centres — government-run early childhood development centres serving children under 6 and pregnant/lactating mothers. Key interventions include toilet block rehabilitation, handwashing station installation, and boundary wall construction.
