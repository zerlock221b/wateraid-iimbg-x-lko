Policy Interventions

Advocating for institutional change and local governance support

Policy interventions involve working beyond engineering — engaging with municipal authorities, community leaders, and government bodies to create lasting systemic change for WASH infrastructure.

Engaging municipal corporation officials to secure government funding for boundary wall construction at Anganwadi Centres — currently outside WaterAid's direct project budget.
